How It Works

Get started in four simple steps and make a difference in your community today.

01

Spot the Issue

Notice a pothole, broken streetlight, or any civic problem? Identify issues that need attention in your neighborhood.

02

Submit Your Report

Capture a photo, add a description, and let our system automatically tag the location. Submit your report in under a minute.

03

Authorities Respond

Your report is immediately routed to the relevant municipal department. They review and prioritize based on severity.

04

Issue Resolved

Track the progress from pending to in-progress to resolved. Receive updates and confirmation when the issue is fixed.
